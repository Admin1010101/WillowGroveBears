· Straight answers

Questions, handled

When does the season start? +
The season starts July 20th. Registration closes July 15th so we can finalize the roster and gear.
What's the deadline to sign up? +
July 15th. Spots are limited and fill up — reserving early with a deposit is the surest way to hold your place.
Does my child need football experience? +
Not at all. All kids are welcome — our coaches teach the game from the ground up, starting with the basics.
Who's coaching the team? +
Experienced staff led by Coach Bey, who's been with Willow Grove since 2002, has won multiple championships, and brings varsity high school coaching experience. Player development is his focus.
Do I have to pay everything up front? +
No. A deposit holds your spot, and flexible payment plans let you spread the rest over time.
Do I need all the paperwork ready to sign up? +
No. Sign up, pay your deposit, and upload just one document (like a birth certificate) to get started. We email you a secure link to finish the rest before July 15.
How do I know if my child qualifies for 12U? +
12U eligibility follows Pop Warner age requirements. If you're unsure, sign up anyway — our staff will confirm eligibility and help with next steps.
